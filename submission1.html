How Do I Submit?

Step By Step Process

Download the Sequoia Format Deck

We believe the best way to communicate an idea is with the Sequoia format. A template of the deck can be found here.

Sign the NDA
‍

The NDA is our commitment to keeping your IP and idea confidential. The document can be found here to review and sign.

Pay the $150 Fee and Submit

Once you've gathered the previous documents, head on over to our Apply page, complete payment, and submit your idea.

We'll Be in Touch!
‍

Our multidisciplinary panel will review submission on a rolling basis. Expect to hear back in 3-4 weeks regarding your advancement to the in-person pitch competition.
